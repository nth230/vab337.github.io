INVENTION Company
A multi-media and multi-talented company
In Vietnamese culture, three years in high school is probably the most beautiful time in a person’s life, where we are carefree and reckless at the same time. I couldn’t be more proud to say my high school years is the turning point in my life, when all of my biggest and proudest achievements were made. And the proudest and biggest of them all is INVENTION Company, more specifically, the fact that I am a member of this amazing group of people.

INVENTION Company is the name of the Facebook page for my high school class. Here we post videos of our performances, our jokes, our funny videos, our memories of high school time when we got to spend 8 hours a day together, in the same room.

INVENTION Company is where I realized how success and pride are created: because of spontaneity and of the people you just absolutely adore.

Just spend a few minutes browsing our videos, and you will get a sense of how special a class we were! And you will probably realize that there is really nothing we can't do and don’t dare to do.
